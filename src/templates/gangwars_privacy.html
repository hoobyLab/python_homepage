Privacy Policy for Gangwars

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Introduction

Gangwars ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our cross-platform text-based role-playing game. Gangwars is a local-first game: all gameplay data, save files, and preferences remain on your device.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you individually. There are no required accounts or sign-ins to play.

2. Gameplay Data (Local Only)

To provide the gameplay experience, Gangwars stores the following information on your device:

  • Game Progress: Local saves for player name, gang name, money, weapons, inventory, gang members, day count, and high scores.
  • App Preferences: User settings for game options, sound, and display.
  • Usage Analytics: Basic usage patterns to improve app performance.

3. Real-time Multiplayer Chat

If you use the in-game global chat, messages are routed through a real-time WebSocket (Socket.IO) connection so other players in the same location-based chat room can see them. Chat messages are not archived on our servers; they are transient and exist only for the duration of the active session.

4. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: For saving game progress, configuration, and high scores.
  • Network Access: Required only for the optional real-time chat and leaderboard features.

How We Use Your Information

All data processed by Gangwars is used solely to:

  • Provide core gameplay (combat, drug economy, gang management, banking, and exploration).
  • Save and restore your in-game progress.
  • Maintain user preferences and high-score leaderboards.
  • Route optional real-time chat messages between players.

Data Storage and Security

  • Game saves and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Chat messages are transient and are not stored on our servers.
  • No telemetry, advertising IDs, or device fingerprinting is collected.

Third-Party Services

  • No third-party analytics.
  • No advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • The Socket.IO client library is loaded from the cdnjs.cloudflare.com CDN to enable optional real-time chat; no personal data is sent to that CDN.

Children's Privacy

Gangwars contains mature themes (extreme violence, drug use, criminal activity) and is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in this document within the application.
