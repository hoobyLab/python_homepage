Privacy Policy for Dumb Phone

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Introduction

Dumb Phone ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Dumb Phone handles information when you use our Android network security application. Dumb Phone is built with a "Privacy First" architecture: all packet inspection, filtering, and logging logic occurs on-device, and your network traffic data is never sent to external servers.

Information We Process

1. Personal Information

Dumb Phone does not collect any personal information that can identify you individually. There are no accounts, sign-ins, or telemetry sent off your device.

2. Network Data (Local Only)

To provide its firewall functionality, Dumb Phone processes network traffic on your device using the Android VPN Service API. This includes:

  • Connection Logs: Local storage of firewall logs, blocked and allowed connections, and destination metadata (IP, port, protocol, timestamp).
  • Country Blacklists: Local copy of the pre-loaded IP range database used for country-based blocking.
  • Custom Rules: User-defined firewall rules, saved on-device.
  • App Preferences: Settings for firewall modes, rule configuration, and log retention.

3. Process Metadata

When a process triggers a network event, the responsible process/package name may be recorded alongside the log entry so you can audit which apps are communicating.

4. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • VPN Service: To intercept, analyze, and filter network traffic on-device.
  • Storage: To persist firewall logs, rules, and the local IP range database.

How We Use Your Information

All data processed by Dumb Phone is used solely to:

  • Provide real-time network security and firewall functionality.
  • Display connection logs and process activity to you.
  • Apply your custom rules and country blocklists.
  • Maintain your user preferences and configuration.

No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device in secure app storage.
  • No information is transmitted to external servers, including our own.
  • The application does not rely on cloud-based analysis, ensuring protection even in isolated environments.
  • Logs are retained according to your configured retention period (1–30 days) and are automatically purged thereafter.

Third-Party Services

  • No third-party analytics.
  • No data sharing, selling, or renting.
  • No advertising or tracking SDKs.

Zero Data Export

Your network logs and activity data remain strictly within your device's secure storage. There is no built-in mechanism that exports your traffic data off the device, and the application has no cloud backend.

Children's Privacy

Dumb Phone is a security utility and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document within the application.
