# 🧟 Zombie Tim User Guide

Welcome to the official guide for Zombie Tim! This document will help you understand how to interact with your new undead best friend.

## 📱 Interface Overview

### 1. Chat Screen
The main screen of the app is where you'll spend most of your time. 
- **Message Input:** Type your messages at the bottom of the screen.
- **Send Button:** Tap the arrow to send your message to Tim.
- **Tim's Response:** Tim will reply with his unique zombie personality, often incorporating tech jargon.

### 2. Animated Avatars
Both you and Zombie Tim have animated avatars powered by the Flame engine.
- Tim will react to the conversation.
- You can see your own "survivor" avatar as well.

### 3. Settings
Access the settings via the gear icon.
- **Personality Tweaks:** (Coming soon) Adjust how "zombie-like" or "tech-savvy" Tim is.
- **Theme Selection:** Switch between different undead aesthetics.

## 🧠 Features

### AI Chat
Tim is powered by an AI model trained to be a tech-enthusiast zombie. He knows about:
- Flutter and Dart
- Linux and Open Source
- Hardware hacking
- ...and brains (mostly as a metaphor for CPU power).

### Dictionary & Passive Learning
Tim loves words! 
- **Dictionary:** Access a tech-focused dictionary.
- **Passive Learning:** Tim will occasionally suggest new tech terms for you to learn while you chat.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

### Tim isn't responding
- Ensure you have an active internet connection if using an online AI provider.
- Check the settings to ensure the service is correctly configured.

### App is slow
- The Flame engine can be resource-intensive on older devices. Try closing other background apps.

## 🧟 Stay Spooky!
Enjoy your time with Zombie Tim. Remember, he's more afraid of your bugs than you are of his bite!
