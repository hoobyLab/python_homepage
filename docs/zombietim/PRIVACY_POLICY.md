# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026

## Introduction

Zombie Tim ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We do not collect any personal information that can identify you individually.

### 2. App Usage Data
- **Dictionary Words and Definitions**: We collect and store word definitions from public domain sources for offline dictionary functionality.
- **App Preferences**: Local storage of user preferences and settings.
- **Usage Analytics**: Basic usage patterns to improve app performance.

### 3. Internet Access
The app requires internet access solely for:
- Downloading dictionary data during initial setup
- Updating word definitions when available
- No personal data is transmitted or collected

## Data Storage and Security

- All dictionary data is stored locally on your device
- No personal information is transmitted to external servers
- App preferences are stored locally using secure storage methods
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

## Third-Party Services

- We do not use third-party analytics services
- We do not share your data with any external parties
- No advertising or tracking services are integrated

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Data Retention

- Dictionary data is retained as long as the app is installed
- User preferences are retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
